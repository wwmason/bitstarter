Sharing: it's one of the first things that children learn in school. Think back -- those were happy times, weren't they? Fingerpainting. Play-time. Almost everyone was friendly. mayiborrow.it wants to bring that feeling back -- regardless of age.

Have you ever tried to borrow a new release from the library? You're #12 of 14 holds. Sound familiar? At that rate, you should be able to get it in 4 months (give or take -- depending on how many people renew). You could go out and buy it, but new hardcovers, albums, or movies aren't exactly cheap. Besides, you just want to check the darn thing out -- you don't know if you want to own it yet! Chances are, in your community, lots of folks already have the thing, and it's just collecting dust! What are the odds that they'd lend it to you? Let's find out!

FAQ

Can't I already do this with something like Craigslist?
Sure you can, but ...

Don't get me wrong: Craigslist is great. It's also pretty impersonal. How do you know the person you want to lend to or from won't return your stuff covered with coffee-stains (and isn't a total creeper)? mayiborrow.it couples the location-specific goodness of Craigslist with an eBay-esque personal rating system so that you can make connections with reliable people near you.

What'll it cost me?
The plan is that it'll cost you nothing. mayiborrow.it will be ad-supported. There'll be an option to pay a small monthly or yearly fee to have an ad-free experience, but that will be completely optional.
Why do you need funding? And why from us?
A couple reasons:
  • I don't want to just throw something completely unpolished together. I want mayiborrow.it to be useful from day one. I'd like to dedicate a solid chunk of time to just focusing on getting mayiborrow.it up and running.
  • I don't anticipate those angel investors or big venture capital firms to be interested in a project like this. To be fair to them, I don't anticipate that there will be much money in it. But things can be extremely useful without turning profound profits: I don't think lined paper is pulling in as much money as say, iPhones, but it's still pretty useful stuff! The goal is to simply have the ads pay for the bandwidth and hosting.
  • I don't really want money from angels or VCs (no offense, guys!). The goal here is to build and strengthen local communities and the bonds between inhabitants. I think if folks that are planning on using mayiborrow.it chip in, it'll foster a sense of ownership, and I think that's a fundamental part of community-building.
What will backing mayiborrow.it get me?
Let's plan to do this public radio-style with two tiers:
  • If you give mayiborrow.it less than $10US, you'll get our eternal gratitude memorialized on a thank-you page on mayiborrow.it
  • If you give mayiborrow.it $10US or more, you'll get an ad-free membership to mayiborrow.it for the lifetime of the site (as well as the aforementioned thank-you page shout-out).
